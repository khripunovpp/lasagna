# 📝 Drafts

The **Drafts** tab controls how the app handles unfinished form changes (recipes, products, invoices) that you didn't explicitly save.

## 🔁 Save drafts

A switch labeled **Save drafts**.

- **On** *(default)* — when you leave a form with unsaved changes, those changes are kept locally as a draft. You can return to them later from the drafts banner that appears on top of recipe / product / invoice lists.
- **Off** — the app **does not store unsaved form changes** locally. Leaving a form discards anything you didn't save explicitly.

A small ❓ next to the switch repeats the same hint.

## ⏳ Keep drafts for, days

A number field that controls how long a draft is kept before it expires.

- The value is in days. The allowed range is shown in the ❓ hint next to the field.
- After the configured period, a draft gets a **24-hour deletion warning**, and is then removed automatically.
- This keeps the drafts list from growing forever with stale entries you'll never come back to.

> 💡 Both settings are saved automatically a moment after you change them — there's no separate Save button.
