# 🏠 Home Page

After launching the app, you land on the **Home Page** (titled *"Welcome"*) — your starting screen and the main entry point to all key features.

## What's on this page:

- 📣 **Promo widgets** — short announcements from the team (e.g., new features, the Telegram Mini App). They appear at the top and can be dismissed.
- 🚀 **Onboarding** — a short guided checklist shown until you've completed the first basic steps.
- 🕘 **Latest updates** — a card titled *"Latest updates"* with two sub-blocks:
  - **Recipes** — most recently edited recipes (each item shows a name and how long ago it was changed).
  - **Products** — same, for products.
  - Clicking an entry opens it for editing. The block is hidden if you have no records yet.

## Top Navigation Bar:

The header is fixed at the top of the screen and grouped into sections from left to right:

- ⬅️ **Back** — appears only when you have a previous screen to return to.
- 🥬 **Logo** — opens the Home Page from anywhere in the app. A small dot appears on the logo when you have unread promo widgets.
- 🔎 **Global search** — full-text search across recipes, products, and invoices.
- **Main nav** (the pill in the middle):
  - **Recipes** — your recipe list.
  - **Storage** — all saved products.
  - **Invoices** — invoice list.
- ⚙️ **Settings** — language, currency, categories, taxes, credentials, backups.
- 🧩 **Widgets** — utility tools (currently the Gelatin Converter).
- ❓ **Documentation** — opens this in-app documentation.
