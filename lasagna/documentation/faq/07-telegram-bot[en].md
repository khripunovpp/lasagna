:::accordion Is there a Telegram version of the app?
Yes! Lasagna is available as a Telegram Mini App. You can use it directly inside Telegram without installing anything extra.

👉 [Open in Telegram](https://t.me/lasagnacost_bot)
:::

:::accordion What can I do in the Telegram Mini App?
Everything you can do in the web app — manage recipes, calculate costs, track ingredients — all without leaving Telegram.
:::
